About Us

We are a husband-and-wife duo building Books and Seasons around the things we love most: good stories, seasonal living, and a slower, more intentional way of life. We created this space to share that world and inspire fellow readers.

Robin

Design & Development

With a background in graphic design and illustration and a deep interest in technology, Robin combines design and development to shape the Books and Seasons platform.

Stephy

The Creative Writer

With a bachelor’s degree in English literature and a keen eye for research, Stephy is the voice behind much of the content on Books and Seasons. She brings stories to life through thoughtful writing and creative storytelling.
