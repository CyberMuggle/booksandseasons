Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Books and Seasons ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our website and platform.

Books and Seasons operates from Belgium and processes personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create an account or interact with our platform, we may collect information such as:

  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Book lists, reading activity, ratings, and reviews
  • Messages or communication sent through the platform

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically through cookies and analytics tools.

  • Anonymized IP address (collected through analytics tools)
  • Browser and device information
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain the Books and Seasons platform
  • Allow users to create accounts and manage reading activity
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Understand how visitors use the platform
  • Detect security issues and prevent abuse
  • Communicate with users when necessary

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, we process personal data based on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Consent – for cookies, analytics, and optional services
  • Contract – to provide and maintain user accounts
  • Legitimate interests – to improve and secure the platform
  • Legal obligations – where required by applicable law

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to support login functionality, analyze traffic, and support advertising and affiliate partnerships.

For detailed information, please see our Cookie Policy.

5. Analytics

We may use analytics services such as Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website.

Google Analytics collects anonymized information about traffic, behavior, and device information. We use IP anonymization so that full IP addresses are not stored by our analytics system.

6. Advertising

Books and Seasons may display advertisements through advertising partners such as Raptive. Advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to measure ad performance and deliver relevant advertising.

7. Affiliate Programs

Our website may contain affiliate links to book retailers and other services, including the Amazon Associates Program.

If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may receive a commission. These programs may use cookies to track referrals.

8. Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be shared with trusted service providers where necessary to operate the platform, including:

  • Hosting providers
  • Analytics providers
  • Advertising partners
  • Security services

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the services of the platform or comply with legal obligations.

Account data is typically retained while an account remains active or until a user requests deletion.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

11. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you may have the following rights:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

12. Children's Privacy

Books and Seasons is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our services.

Updates will be posted on this page.

14. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us through the contact information provided on our website.
