Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 2026

Books and Seasons is committed to making our website and platform as accessible and usable as possible for all visitors, including people with disabilities.

We aim to design and maintain our website with accessibility in mind and to continually improve the experience for everyone who visits Books and Seasons.

1. Our Commitment

We want our content, features, and services to be accessible to as many people as possible. This includes readers using assistive technologies such as screen readers, keyboard navigation, voice control, and other accessibility tools.

2. Accessibility Measures

We work to support accessibility by following good design and development practices, including where possible:

  • Using clear page structure and semantic HTML
  • Providing logical heading order
  • Supporting keyboard navigation
  • Maintaining readable color contrast
  • Using descriptive link text where appropriate
  • Adding alternative text for meaningful images where appropriate
  • Designing content to be readable across modern devices and browsers

3. Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. As Books and Seasons grows, we will continue reviewing, testing, and improving our website and platform to identify and reduce accessibility barriers.

Some parts of the site may not yet be fully optimized for every user, every device, or every assistive technology. We are committed to improving those areas over time.

4. Third-Party Content and Services

Some parts of our website may include third-party tools, embedded content, advertisements, or linked external websites. We cannot control the accessibility of third-party services, but we aim to work with services that support accessible experiences whenever reasonably possible.

5. Feedback

If you experience accessibility barriers while using Books and Seasons, we encourage you to contact us and let us know. Feedback helps us improve the site.

When contacting us, it is helpful to include:

  • The page URL or feature where you encountered the issue
  • A description of the problem
  • The device, browser, or assistive technology you were using, if relevant

6. Contact

If you need assistance or would like to report an accessibility issue, please contact us through the contact information provided on our website.
