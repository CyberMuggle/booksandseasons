Community Guidelines

Last updated: April 2026

Books and Seasons is a community for readers who want to discover books, share reading experiences, and connect with others who love literature. To keep the platform welcoming, thoughtful, and safe for everyone, we ask all members to follow these community guidelines.

1. Be Respectful

Respect other members of the community. Healthy discussion and different opinions about books are welcome, but personal attacks, harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior are not allowed.

  • No harassment or bullying
  • No hate speech or discrimination
  • No threats or abusive language

2. Honest Reviews and Opinions

Reviews and ratings should reflect your genuine reading experience. Please avoid posting misleading, promotional, or dishonest reviews.

  • Do not post fake reviews
  • Clearly disclose if you received a book for free
  • Avoid promotional spam disguised as reviews

3. No Spam or Self-Promotion

Books and Seasons is designed for readers and thoughtful discussion. Excessive self-promotion, unsolicited advertising, or repetitive promotional content is not allowed.

  • No spam links
  • No repeated promotional posts
  • No automated or bot-generated content

4. Respect Intellectual Property

Only share content that you have the right to share. Do not upload or distribute copyrighted material without permission.

  • Do not upload pirated books
  • Do not share copyrighted content without permission

5. Protect Privacy

Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information about other users without their consent.

  • No sharing of private contact information
  • No doxxing or exposure of personal data

6. Reporting Issues

If you encounter behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it to us. Reporting helps us keep the community safe and welcoming.

7. Enforcement

Books and Seasons reserves the right to remove content or restrict accounts that violate these guidelines. Depending on the severity of the violation, actions may include content removal, warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent account removal.

8. Changes to These Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines from time to time to reflect changes to the platform or community needs. Updates will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about these guidelines, please contact us using the contact information available on our website.
