Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Books and Seasons ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can control them.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide information to the website owners about how the site is used.

Cookies can be either:

  • Session cookies – deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies – remain on your device for a defined period

2. How We Use Cookies

Books and Seasons uses cookies to operate the website, provide account functionality, understand how visitors use our platform, and support advertising and affiliate partnerships that help fund the site.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are required for the website to function properly. These cookies enable features such as user login, account authentication, and security protection. Because these cookies are necessary for the operation of the site, they cannot be disabled.

Examples include cookies used for:

  • User login sessions
  • Account authentication
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Remembering basic user preferences

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies help us measure traffic, understand which pages are popular, and improve the overall experience of the platform.

Our website may use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, to collect information such as page visits, session duration, device type, and general geographic region. The data collected through these cookies helps us understand how our site is used and how we can improve it.

Advertising Cookies

Our website may display advertisements through advertising partners such as Raptive. Advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to deliver relevant advertisements and measure advertising performance.

Advertising cookies may track interactions with advertisements, measure campaign effectiveness, and help prevent the same advertisement from being shown too frequently.

Affiliate Tracking Cookies

Some links on our website are affiliate links. When you click these links, a cookie may be placed on your device so that the affiliate partner can recognize that the referral came from our website.

We may participate in affiliate programs such as the Amazon Associates Program and other similar programs. These cookies allow affiliate partners to track purchases made after clicking an affiliate link so that we may receive a small commission.

These cookies do not increase the price you pay and help support the continued operation of our website.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies improve the usability of the site by remembering certain preferences and settings, such as interface preferences or display settings.

4. Third‑Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third‑party services that appear on our pages. These third parties may collect information about your interaction with our website.

Examples of third‑party services we may use include:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Raptive for advertising services
  • Amazon Associates and other affiliate networks for referral tracking

These third‑party services have their own privacy and cookie policies which may change from time to time.

5. Cookie Duration

Cookies may remain on your device for different periods of time depending on their purpose.

  • Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies remain until they expire or are deleted

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through your browser settings.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website, including login features and personalized experiences.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our services. Any updates will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy, you may contact us through the contact information provided on our website.
